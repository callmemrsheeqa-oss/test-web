 About Our Department

📚 Department Introduction

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Excellence was established in 1995 with the vision of producing world-class computing professionals. Over the past three decades, the department has grown into one of the most respected institutions in the region.

We offer a diverse range of programs at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels, covering areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and Computer Networks. Our curriculum is regularly updated to match industry demands.

With a team of highly qualified faculty, cutting-edge laboratories, and strong industry linkages, we prepare students not just for jobs, but for leadership roles in the technology sector.

🎯 Vision & Mission
Our Vision
To be a globally recognized center of excellence in Computer Science education and research.


Our Mission

  • To deliver high-quality, industry-relevant education in computing disciplines.

  • To foster research and innovation that addresses real-world challenges.

  • To nurture ethical, skilled, and socially responsible computing professionals.

  • To build strong collaborations with industry, government, and international institutions.

  • To provide an inclusive and supportive learning environment for all students.

✨ Core Values
  1. Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in everything we do.

  2. Integrity: Acting honestly and ethically at all times.

  3. Innovation: Embracing new ideas, technologies, and approaches.

  4. Inclusivity: Welcoming diversity and equal opportunity for all.

  5. Collaboration: Working together for shared success and growth.
