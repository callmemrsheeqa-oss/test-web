  🔒 Lab 3 — Networking & Cybersecurity Lab    [Capacity: 30 Students]

Designed for hands-on network configuration, ethical hacking labs, and security research in a safe, isolated environment.

Equipment & Tools:
  • 8 x Cisco Catalyst 9300 Series Managed Switches
  • 6 x Cisco ISR 4000 Series Routers
  • 2 x Palo Alto Next-Generation Firewall Appliances
  • 30 x Kali Linux Workstations for penetration testing
  • Software: Wireshark, Nmap, Metasploit, Packet Tracer, GNS3
  • Fully isolated lab network (no external internet access)
